Get To Know Arby

Hey, I'm Arby! I've spent 10 years behind the chair mastering the art of color, cuts, and transformation. Based in [City], I specialize in blondes, balayage, and lived-in color that looks as good months down the line as it does on day one.

My chair is a safe space—a place where we collaborate on a vision that fits your lifestyle, your face shape, and your personal aesthetic. I don't just 'do hair', I engineer confidence.

Beyond the chair, I'm an educator, content creator, and entrepreneur passionate about elevating the craft and the stylists who love it. Whether I'm teaching a room of 50 stylists or painting a single head of foils, the goal is always the same: excellence.

"To build confidence through artistry, and to push the boundaries of what is possible in the modern salon experience."
